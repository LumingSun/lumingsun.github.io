Biography

Luming Sun (孙路明) receieved his PhD degree from Renmin University of China, under the supervision of professor Cuiping Li. His area of research is artificial intelligence for systems (AI4Sys), and he is particularly interested in providing traditional database systems with practical and reliable machine learning techniques for better performance (AI4DB). He maintains a paper list (ML4DB-paper-list) about the topic for people who might be interested in.
